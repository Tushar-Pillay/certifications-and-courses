## 📘 Description
This Letter of Appreciation was awarded for active participation in the
**SQL-Wordle Research Study 1**, conducted online by the **Centre for Educational
Technology, IIT Bombay**.

The study focused on evaluating the effectiveness of **game-based learning**
approaches to enhance SQL skill development. Participation involved practicing
SQL concepts through the SQL-Wordle online game as part of an academic research
initiative.
