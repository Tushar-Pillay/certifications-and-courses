## 📘 Course Description
This course introduces the practical use of **Artificial Intelligence (ChatGPT)**
alongside **Microsoft Excel** to automate, accelerate, and enhance data-related
tasks. It focuses on standardizing, extracting, classifying, and generating data,
particularly when working with unstructured datasets.

The course demonstrates how **prompt engineering and AI-driven workflows**
can significantly reduce manual effort and improve analytical productivity
in business environments.
