## 📘 Course Description
This introductory skill badge validates hands-on experience in **prompt
engineering and generative AI** using **Google Cloud Vertex AI**. The course
focuses on designing effective prompts to guide large language and multimodal
models for accurate, relevant, and high-quality outputs.

It also introduces **image analysis and multimodal generative techniques** and
demonstrates how **Gemini models** can be applied to real-world business and
marketing scenarios.
