## 📚 Curriculum & Key Learnings
The certification journey consisted of five core modules designed to build a professional foundation in AI:

### 1. Introduction to AI
*   Explored the history of AI and its current impact on industries like healthcare, finance, and retail.
*   Learned the fundamental difference between classical programming and AI-driven systems.

### 2. Machine Learning & Deep Learning
*   Gained insights into how machines learn from data through **Supervised**, **Unsupervised**, and **Reinforcement Learning**.
*   Explored the structure of **Neural Networks** and how Deep Learning mimics human cognitive functions to solve complex problems.

### 3. NLP & Computer Vision
*   **Natural Language Processing:** Understanding how AI processes, translates, and generates human language (chatbots, voice assistants).
*   **Computer Vision:** Learning how machines "see" by classifying images and recognizing patterns in visual data.

### 4. Running AI Models (IBM Watson Studio)
*   Acquired a conceptual understanding of how to build and deploy models using professional tools like [IBM Watson Studio](https://ibm.com).
*   Practiced navigating the tech stack: Data preparation, Model training, and Deployment.

### 5. AI Ethics
*   Studied the critical importance of **Fairness**, **Accountability**, and **Transparency**.
*   Examined the risks of data bias and the responsibility of developers to create ethical AI solutions.
