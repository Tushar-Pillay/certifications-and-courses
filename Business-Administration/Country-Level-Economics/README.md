## 📘 Course Description
This course provides a comprehensive introduction to **macroeconomic analysis**
at the country level. It focuses on understanding how key macroeconomic variables
interact with markets, financial systems, and economic policy to shape national
and global economic outcomes.

The course emphasizes applying economic reasoning to real-world business,
financial, and policy decisions, making it highly relevant for business
administration, analytics, and strategic planning roles.

- Market dynamics and economic indicators
