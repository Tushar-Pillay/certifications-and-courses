## 📘 Course Description
This course provides a comprehensive understanding of **supply chain management**
from an end-to-end learning perspective. It focuses on how organizations design,
coordinate, and optimize supply chains to improve operational efficiency and
deliver value to customers.

The course emphasizes the integration of logistics, supplier management,
quality processes, and continuous improvement to support effective
decision-making in complex business environments.
