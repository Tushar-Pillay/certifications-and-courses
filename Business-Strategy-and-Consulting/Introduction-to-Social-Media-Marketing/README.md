## 📘 Course Description
This course provides a comprehensive introduction to **social media marketing
strategy** within the broader digital marketing landscape. It covers how
traditional, digital, and social media channels support business and brand goals.

The course emphasizes **goal setting, KPI definition, audience targeting,
and platform selection**, enabling data-driven marketing and strategic decisions.
