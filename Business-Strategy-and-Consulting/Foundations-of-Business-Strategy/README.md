## 📘 Course Description
This course provides a strong foundation in **business strategy and competitive
analysis**. It focuses on understanding how firms achieve and sustain competitive
advantage by analyzing industries, competitors, and internal capabilities.

The course emphasizes structured strategic thinking and analytical frameworks
used in consulting and senior management decision-making.
