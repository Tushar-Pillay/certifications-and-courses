## 📘 Course Description
This course focuses on developing **creative and structured problem-solving
skills** applicable to business, consulting, and real-world decision-making.
It emphasizes generating innovative ideas, evaluating alternatives, and
approaching complex problems with an open and growth-oriented mindset.

The course strengthens the ability to think creatively while maintaining
analytical rigor, a key skill for strategy and consulting roles.
