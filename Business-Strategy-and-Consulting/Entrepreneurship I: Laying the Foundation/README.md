## 📘 Course Description
This course provides a strong foundation in **entrepreneurship and new venture
creation**, focusing on how entrepreneurs identify opportunities, evaluate
market viability, and build scalable business ideas.

The course emphasizes strategic thinking, leadership, and decision-making skills
required to transform ideas into viable ventures, making it highly relevant for
strategy, consulting, and entrepreneurial roles.
