## 📘 Program Description
The McKinsey.org Forward Program is a structured learning experience designed to
build **practical workplace and consulting skills** essential for professional
growth in business, analytics, and strategy-oriented roles.

The program emphasizes structured problem-solving, effective communication,
adaptability, and applying business frameworks to real-world professional
challenges.
